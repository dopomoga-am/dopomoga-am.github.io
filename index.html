Ukrainian and Artsakh refugee aid Center in Armenia

Dopomoga.am is a humanitarian project helping refugees from Ukraine and Artsakh. Join us in our quest to make the world a better place for those who find themselves in difficult situations.

What we do

  • collect and distribute humanitarian aid
  • distribute used clothes
  • provide informational and juristic assistance

If you came from Ukraine and you need help, call to this number: (+374) 44 301 339 from 10:00 till 19:00

Dopomoga.am it`s also a space of mutual support. Together we watch movies, learn languages, do sports, communicate and discover interesting things. Come to us at 35 Khanjyan st. to become part of our warm and friendly community.
